## /the-making
This is the part that doesn't exist yet and will take the most work.

The plan is to build a second path through this repo for people who don't want to read the show — they want to understand how it was built. The chime arc. The camera arc. Why Bangkok. Why this structure. What got cut and why. What the producers said and what Jay kept anyway.

Not "here's how to write a good show." Just: here's how this one got built. Draw your own conclusions.
