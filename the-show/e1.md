INT. KHLONG TOEI BUILDING — LOBBY — CONTINUOUS
Dark. Humid. A security guard nods at Met.
Met stands in the center. Looks up. Looks down. He touches a wall. Runs his finger along a crack. Stops. Presses. Walks to the stairwell. Counts steps. Knocks on the floor with his heel. Listens.
[camera grammar on Met's eyes / building POV — removed]

INT. KHLONG TOEI BUILDING — THIRD FLOOR — CONTINUOUS
Met moving faster. He stops in a hallway. Chloe behind him. He starts talking — to himself, or to his phone where Ice is listening.
MET
Column shifted post-construction. Probably the '11 floods. Load redistributed to the east wall, which shouldn't hold but does because someone — accidentally, I'm sure — poured the foundation six inches deeper than spec on that side. The crack is cosmetic. Lateral stress indicators are within tolerance if you account for the soil subsidence pattern, which nobody did because nobody looked at the geotechnical from '09.
He touches the wall. Almost gentle.
MET (CONT'D)
Building's not going to fall down.
(beat)
It just doesn't know why it's standing up.

EXT. KHLONG TOEI BUILDING — STREET — DAY
Outside. Met on the phone with Ice, dictating the report. Chloe beside him reviewing data.

Met picks it up without breaking stride. Doesn't react.
CHLOE
(staring)
Did you see —

See what?

CHLOE

[center point reveal / camera on cabinets — removed]

INT. OFFICE STAIRWELL — EVENING
Chloe's phone BUZZES. She steps out. Finds the stairwell. Sits on a step.

She looks at the keychain. Puts it in her pocket.
She walks back in. Sits down. Pulls up the map.
---
INT. MET'S OFFICE — EVENING
Ice alone. She opens the THICK FILING CABINET. Met's file — decades thick. She cross-references the report against the thick file. Makes a phone call. Thai, fast. Maintaining something.
[Ice muttering dates / Chloe overhearing / filing cabinet thickness note — removed]

(Subtitled: "Two thousand seventeen... no, two thousand sixteen...")

ICE
(to herself, barely audible)
Dai yen yen ka ka kaaa.

INT. BUNGALOW — UNKNOWN — DREAM
Not soft focus. One place. Vivid. Real. Sharper than the waking world.
The bungalow — but full of color and life. Toys on the floor. Drawings on the fridge. Tiny rain boots by the door. Puppy UNIT scrambling across the floor.
Met in the bathroom. Shaving. His eyes are different. Lighter. Present.

INT. MET'S BUNGALOW — BEDROOM — NIGHT

His hand reaching toward the wall. Toward a space where a drawing used to be.
MET
(whisper, almost involuntary)

Unit at the end of the hallway. Looking at the corner.
Met goes back to bed. Stares at the ceiling. Won't sleep again.
Unit stays in the hallway. Looking at the corner.

EXT. SUKHUMVIT ROAD — MORNING

Through the glass: Chloe is already at the spare desk. Her map has more points now.
She looks up when Met enters. Nods. Goes back to work.
Met sits at his desk. Opens a new case file.
